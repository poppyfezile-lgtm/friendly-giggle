About Poppy Bhekzile

Background

I'm a professional focused on helping others navigate AI with clarity and precision. Rather than chasing trends, I provide grounded, practical guidance rooted in real-world applications.

Philosophy

AI is powerful, but most information about it is either oversimplified or inaccessible. My approach:

  • No hype: Only evidence-based, conservative claims.
  • No jargon: Clear language accessible to professionals at any level.
  • Practical focus: Actionable insights you can apply immediately.
  • Sector-aware: Tailored guidance for your specific industry.

What I Do

I help professionals and organizations understand AI tools, navigate policy considerations, explore educational opportunities, and implement AI effectively in their work. Through consulting and guidance, I translate complex AI concepts into clear, actionable strategies.
